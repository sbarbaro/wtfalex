Island Challenge!

The island challenge is a rite of passage for trainers. Once trainers turn 11 they can chose to patake in the island challege, where trainers travel to the four islands of alola to complete various challenges. At the end of each challenge the trainers must fight a powerful Totem Pokemon!

The final trail on the island is the grand trial, where the trainer must face off against the island kahuna! Once the grand trial is completed the trainers can go on to the next island. The goal of the island challenge is to become the island challenge champion!
