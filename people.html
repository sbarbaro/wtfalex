People

There are a lot of important people in Alola that are definitely worth your time to meet!

Professor Kukui

Professor Kukui is Alola's very own Pokemon professor, as well as the creator of Alola's Pokemon League! He specializes in moves, so if you need help with what moves to teach your /pokemon /kukui is the professor to visit!

Trial captains

Each island has 1 to 3 trial captions that trainers need to face off against to beat the Island challenge! Pictures of them can be found in the Gallery.

Kahunas

Each island has a Kahuna. Kanunas preside over their respective island's Grand Challenge. Pictures of them can be found in the Gallery.
